## 📊 Day 8 Key Findings

**Bank Customer Churn Analysis (10,000 customers)**

| Finding | Retained | Churned | Insight |
|---------|----------|---------|---------|
| **Avg Age** | 37 years | 45 years | Older customers churn more |
| **Avg Balance** | €72,745 | €91,109 | Richer customers churn more |
| **Churn Rate** | 79.63% | 20.37% | 1 in 5 customers leave |
| **Credit Score** | 651 | 645 | Doesn't predict churn |
| **Tenure** | 5 years | 5 years | Loyalty time doesn't help |

**Recommendation:** Target retention programs at customers 45+ with high balances.
